Tang Yuan (汤圆)

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous rice flour
  • ½ cup warm water
  • Optional filling: black sesame + sugar + fat

Sweet Soup

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 slices ginger
  • Rock or brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Make dough, wrap filling, form balls.
  2. Boil until floating + 2 min.
  3. Serve in sweet ginger soup.
