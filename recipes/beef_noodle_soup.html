Beef Noodle Soup (牛肉面)

Home

Noodle Dough

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water

Soup Base

  • 1 lb beef shank
  • 4 slices ginger
  • 2 scallions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 star anise
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Make and rest dough.
  2. Simmer soup 2 hours with beef.
  3. Hand-pull noodles and boil fresh.
