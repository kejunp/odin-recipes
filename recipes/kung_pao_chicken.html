Kung Pao Chicken (宫保鸡丁)

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 8 dried red chilies
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 inch ginger
  • ¼ cup peanuts

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorn powder
  • 1 tsp cornstarch + water

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken, mix sauce.
  2. Stir-fry chilies, garlic, and chicken.
  3. Add sauce and peanuts. Stir to thicken.
