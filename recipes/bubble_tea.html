Bubble Tea (珍珠奶茶)

Home

Ingredients

  • ½ cup tapioca starch
  • ¼ cup boiling water
  • Black tea, milk, brown sugar syrup

Instructions

  1. Form and cook pearls. Soak in syrup.
  2. Mix with tea and milk. Serve iced.
