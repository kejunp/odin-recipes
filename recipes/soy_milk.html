Soy Milk (豆浆)

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried soybeans
  • 4 cups water
  • Sugar to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak beans overnight. Blend and strain.
  2. Simmer 20 min. Add sugar if desired.
