Garlic Stir-Fried Greens (蒜蓉小白菜)

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 lb bok choy or gai lan
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Blanch greens briefly.
  2. Sauté garlic in oil.
  3. Stir-fry with greens and salt.
