Honey Lemon Tea (柠檬蜂蜜茶)

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon
  • 2–3 tbsp honey
  • 2 cups water
  • Optional: mint or goji berries

Instructions

  1. Boil herbs (if using), then let cool.
  2. Add lemon and honey. Serve warm or cold.
