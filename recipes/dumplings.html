Pork and Chive Dumplings (饺子)

Home

Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup warm water

Filling

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 cup Chinese chives
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp white pepper

Instructions

  1. Make dough, rest 30 min.
  2. Mix filling, wrap dumplings.
  3. Boil or pan-fry to cook.
